# Bundled Office Assets

CLI_Pixelverse keeps its default UI self-contained. Bundled office furniture
uses selected 32x32 crops from the following free tileset.

Bundled free furniture source:

- Modern Interior Tileset by AppleDog
- Author: Apple Dog
- Source: https://apple-dog.itch.io/modern-tileset-by-appledog
- Author terms shown on itch.io comments: free for commercial/non-commercial projects with Apple Dog credited

The bundled crops cover beds, sofas, chairs, desks, tables, file cabinets,
lamps, and terminal-like office equipment. Agent sprites remain separate.

The Kenney RPG Urban assets under `public/assets/kenney-rpg-urban/` include
their own license and attribution files.
